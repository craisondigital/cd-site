Fountain Phone Control

A compact smart relay is wired to your fountain pump. It connects to your WiFi and gives you full on/off control from the free Shelly app on your phone — from anywhere. Set daily schedules, share access with family, and use voice control with Alexa or Google Home.

LED Light Control

A second smart relay controls your fountain's LED lights independently from the pump. Turn lights on for ambiance even when the fountain is off, or set them to automatically activate at sunset whenever the fountain is running. Controlled from the same app.

Local Weather Station

A solar-powered 7-in-1 weather station is installed on your property. It measures wind speed, gusts, temperature, humidity, rain, UV, and barometric pressure — all in real time. When wind exceeds your set threshold, the fountain shuts off automatically to prevent damage. No moving parts, no maintenance.
