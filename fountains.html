The Process

Simple from day one.

We take care of the technical work. You end up with a system that's easy to manage.

01
We assess your existing setup
We evaluate your current fountain, control panel, and wiring. We confirm the manufacturer, model, and power configuration to determine the best integration approach.
02
Hardware installation
We install the smart relay controller at your fountain's control panel and mount the local weather station in an optimal position on your property — clean wiring, no shortcuts.
03
System configuration
We set up or connect to your Home Assistant instance, configure the fountain controller, integrate the weather station, build your dashboard, and set up automations, schedules, and notifications.
04
You take control
We walk you through the dashboard — how to adjust schedules, change your wind threshold, view history, and control the fountain from your phone. It's simple enough for anyone in the household.
