The Problem

The property you love. The doors you can't control.

Large properties have garage doors and rollup doors scattered across buildings — each with its own remote, its own motor, and no way to know what's open from the main house.

What most people deal with

  • Lying in bed wondering if you left the warehouse open
  • A drawer full of remotes — and none of them work from the main house
  • Driving across the property in the dark to check a door
  • No way to let the contractor into the barn when you're not there
  • Five buildings, five different motors, zero coordination

What CraisonDigital builds

  • One app shows every door across every building — open or closed, right now
  • Get an alert the moment any door is left open past your threshold
  • Close the warehouse from your bed. Open the barn from across town.
  • Control garage lights and get obstruction sensor alerts from the app
  • Let anyone into any building with a tap — and get notified when they arrive
  • Works with your existing motors. No ripping anything out.
