The Process

Simple from day one.

We take care of the technical work. You end up with a system that's easy to manage.

01
We assess your existing setup
We evaluate your current gate opener and confirm compatibility. Most standard openers with a Wiegand input work out of the box.
02
Hardware installation
We mount the UHF RFID reader at the right position for your driveway, wire everything cleanly, and connect to your opener. Optional keypad installed if requested.
03
System configuration
We set up or connect to your Home Assistant instance, configure all integrations, build your access dashboard, and set up notifications and logging.
04
You take control
We walk you through the dashboard — adding tags, setting PINs, viewing logs. It's simple enough that anyone in the household can manage it.
