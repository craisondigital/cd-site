How It Works

Three steps to a unified property.

No construction. No ripping anything out. We work with what you have.

01
We walk every acre and learn what you have
Different buildings, different brands, different installers who came and went over the years. We audit every device on your property — what works, what doesn't, and what's been sitting unused because nobody could figure it out.
02
We connect everything into one system
We install a central hub on your property and integrate every device — regardless of brand or age — into a single platform. Your Lutron lights, Ring cameras, Sonos speakers, Rachio irrigation, pool equipment, gate, garage doors — all unified.
03
We build your dashboard around how you live
No generic templates. We build a custom dashboard with only the controls you actually use, set up automations that match your routines, and make sure everyone in the household can use it without a manual. Then we stay — tuning and improving as your needs change.
