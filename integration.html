How It Works

Three steps to a unified property.

No construction. No ripping anything out. No replacing what already works. We build on top of what you have.

01
We walk every acre and learn your property
Different buildings, different brands, different installers who came and went over the years. We audit every smart device on your property — what works, what doesn't, what's been sitting unused because nobody could figure it out. We map every system across every structure: main house, guest house, pool house, barn, gate, and everything in between.
02
We connect everything into one system
We install a central hub on your property that talks to every device — regardless of brand or age. Lights, thermostats, cameras, speakers, vacuums, irrigation, pool equipment, gate, garage doors, locks — all integrated into a single platform. Devices that never knew each other existed are suddenly working together.
03
We build it around how you actually live
No generic templates. We build a custom dashboard with only the controls you actually use, set up automations that match your daily routines, and make sure everyone in the household can use it. Then we stay — tuning, refining, and adding new devices as your needs evolve. Your system gets smarter over time, not more complicated.
