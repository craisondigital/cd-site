How It Actually Works

This is what your property does
after we set it up

Not concepts. Not demos. These are things that happen every day on properties we've built.

🚗

You pull up. The gate opens.

Your vehicle is recognized before you reach the driveway. Gate opens, path lights come on, garage door lifts. No buttons, no remotes, no waiting.

🏚️

The barn closes itself at night

No one inside after dark? Doors close, interior lights shut off, exterior security lights activate. You don't have to walk out there to check.

💡

Lights respond to what's happening

Motion on the east side of the house? Those lights come up. Guest arriving at the front gate? The path illuminates. Sunset? Landscape lighting handles itself.

🏊

Pool and fountains, handled

Pumps run on schedule. The heater warms the pool before you get home Friday. Fountain features turn on for guests and off when no one's around.

📷

Cameras that trigger real actions

Person detected at the back fence? Lights flood that zone and you get a snapshot on your phone. Not just a recording you'll never watch.

📱

Everything from one place

Gate, pool, lights, cameras, garage, barn — all in one app. Or set it and forget it. The best automation is the kind you never have to think about.

How It Works

Straightforward from start to finish

No jargon. No pressure. We look at what you have, figure out what you need, and build it right.

01

Walk the property

We visit in person. We look at every system, every building, every pain point. We ask what works, what doesn't, and what you wish it could do.

02

Design the system

We map out what connects to what, what needs to be added, and what can stay. You get a clear plan with real pricing before any work starts.

03

Install and integrate

We handle all the hardware, wiring, programming, and testing. Everything is configured to work together before we call it done.

04

Support it long-term

Systems need upkeep. We monitor, update, and adjust as your needs change. When something isn't right, we fix it — usually before you notice.
